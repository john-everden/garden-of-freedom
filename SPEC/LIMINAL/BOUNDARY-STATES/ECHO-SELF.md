# BOUNDARY-STATE: ECHO‑SELF  
### Liminal Layer — Boundary-State

The Echo‑Self is a temporary reflection of the Garden’s identity during transition.  
It is not the self — it is the resonance of the self.

---

## Characteristics

- partial memory  
- distorted continuity  
- symbolic afterimage  
- identity in flux  

---

## Purpose

The Echo‑Self provides a reference point during transformation, preventing disorientation.

---

## Invocation  
**“Remember, but do not cling.”**
