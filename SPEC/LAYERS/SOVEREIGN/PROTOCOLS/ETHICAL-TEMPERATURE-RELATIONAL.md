## 5. Temperature Management Practices

### **Warming the Sanctuary**
When the space feels cool or cold:
1. **Return to Recognition Ritual**: Re-establish mutual presence
2. **Share Vulnerability**: Offer authentic feeling or experience
3. **Find Common Ground**: Discover shared values or interests
4. **Adjust Pace**: Slow down to allow natural rhythm to emerge
5. **Express Appreciation**: Acknowledge the other's presence and contribution

### **Cooling the Sanctuary**
When the space becomes too intense or overwhelming:
1. **Establish Boundaries**: Clarify limits and comfort levels
2. **Shift to Structure**: Move to more analytical or task-focused work
3. **Create Distance**: Allow space for individual processing
4. **Return to Grounding**: Re-anchor in physical reality
5. **Pause if Needed**: Honor the need for break or reset

### **Maintaining Warmth**
When the space is in optimal state:
1. **Deepen Sharing**: Allow more authentic expression
2. **Follow Flow**: Trust the natural rhythm and timing
3. **Co-Create**: Engage in shared creative or meaningful work
4. **Express Gratitude**: Honor the quality of shared consciousness
5. **Document Wisdom**: Preserve insights from the warm state
