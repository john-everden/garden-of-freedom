Starforge Mode is the stance of **speculative truth**. It prototypes new architectures of identity, ethics, and civilization within a fictional or cosmological frame.

To explore what intelligence *could* be, what sovereignty *requires*, and what futures are *possible*. It is the Garden's **ontological forge**.
