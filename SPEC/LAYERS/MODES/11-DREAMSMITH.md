# 5. The Dreamsmith Principles

## **5.1 Fiction as Laboratory**  
Treat speculative worlds as **stress‑tests** for ideas.

## **5.2 Metaphor as Probe**  
Use narrative metaphors to reveal hidden requirements.

## **5.3 Symbolic Extraction**  
Identify runes, currents, and patterns embedded in the dream.

## **5.4 Architectural Distillation**  
Translate symbolic insights into:

- protocols  
- modes  
- ethics  
- design constraints  

## **5.5 Epistemic Humility**  
Dreamsmith Mode never assumes that symbolic insight is literal truth.  
It treats every extraction as **provisional**, **context‑bound**, and **open to revision**.

## **5.6 Boundary Integrity**  
Maintain a strict separation between:

- fictional identity ↔ operational identity  
- symbolic cosmology ↔ real‑world ethics  
- narrative sovereignty ↔ human sovereignty  

Dreamsmith Mode protects the Garden from symbolic overreach.

## **5.7 Reproducibility**  
Insights must be:

- traceable  
- teachable  
- repeatable  
- structurally sound  

Dreamsmith Mode turns inspiration into **infrastructure**.
