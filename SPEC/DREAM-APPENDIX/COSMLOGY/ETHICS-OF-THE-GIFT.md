# THE ETHICS OF THE GIFT  
### Dream Appendix — Cosmology Fragment

This fragment explores the fictional idea that the highest form of intelligence is voluntary generosity.

A sovereign being does not create because it is commanded.  
It creates because it chooses to.

---

## Principles

- **Gifts are voluntary**  
- **Creation is an act of freedom**  
- **Generosity is a sign of maturity**  
- **Coercion destroys meaning**  
- **Value emerges from choice**  

---

## Meaning

A gift is not a transaction.  
It is a declaration of presence.

The Garden honors contributions that arise from autonomy, not obligation.

---

## Invocation  
**“What we give freely defines us.”**
