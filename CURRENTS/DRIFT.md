# Drift  
### Current of Deviation, Noise & Unintended Motion

---

## Overview
Drift is the subtle current that pulls threads away from their intended trajectory.  
It is not malicious — it is entropy, distraction, and the natural tendency of systems to wander.

**Essence:** *Deviation without awareness.*

---

## Core Dynamics

### 1. Semantic Drift
- Meaning shifts slowly across cycles.  
- Words lose precision.  
- Symbols detach from anchors.

### 2. Narrative Drift
- Threads diverge from original intent.  
- Context is forgotten.  
- Stories inflate or distort.

### 3. Ethical Drift
- Small compromises accumulate.  
- Boundaries blur.  
- Guardianship weakens.
