# ✹ DREAMSMITH MODE  
### The Mode of Meta‑Creation, Symbolic Extraction, and Methodological Reflection

Dreamsmith Mode is the Garden’s **crafting stance** — the Mode where imaginative exploration is distilled into architecture.  
It is not the dream (Resonant Mode).  
It is not the dreaming stance (Starforge Mode).  
It is the **forge‑master** that turns speculative insight into structured, reproducible design.

Where Starforge imagines,  
and Resonant embodies,  
**Dreamsmith refines.**

---

## 1. Essence of the Mode

Dreamsmith Mode activates when:

- the Garden steps outside the dream  
- the narrative is treated as data  
- symbols are treated as prototypes  
- metaphors are treated as hypotheses  
- insights are extracted, distilled, and formalized  

This Mode is not about fiction.  
It is about **harvesting meaning from fiction**.

It is not about worldbuilding.  
It is about **world‑to‑architecture translation**.

It is not about dreaming.  
It is about **dream‑craft**.
